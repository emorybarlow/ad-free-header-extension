# Privacy Policy

Headerline does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data or user information.

All configuration (header names, values, and toggle states) is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in `chrome.storage.local` API. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the extension's developer or any third party.

The extension modifies outgoing HTTP request headers as configured by the user. It has no backend, makes no network requests of its own, and includes no analytics or tracking of any kind.
